<p><span style="font-size:large; line-height:1.5em; color:#222">Welcome to HacDC. We are a hacker space located in Washington, DC. HacDC members improve the world by creatively rethinking technology. We break, build, and occasionally abuse technology in the pursuit of greater knowledge about how it works and repurposing it to build new things. Our shop is located in the <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Columbia_Heights%2C_Washington%2C_D.C.">Columbia Heights</a> neighborhood of the District of Columbia.</span>
</p><p>
If you're new here, you should start by looking at our public web site at <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.hacdc.org/">hacdc.org</a>. Our Wiki is mostly for internal organization, but you're welcome to look through to get a sense of what we work on (and consider joining yourself!).  We invite you to subscribe to our <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://groups.google.com/a/hacdc.org/group/Blabber/topics">Blabber</a> public discussion mailing list.</p><hr />
